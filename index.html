Our Mission

Beam Audio is dedicated to advancing fundamental research in digital signal processing, focusing on Sparse Approximation and the Analytic Signal domain. We aim to create robust and semantically manipulable audio codecs that perform efficiently in real-time, enabling new possibilities for interactive audio applications.
